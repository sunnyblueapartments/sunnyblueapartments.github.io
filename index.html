Explore Omiš

Omiš is a charming town on Croatia’s Dalmatian coast, located at the mouth of the Cetina River where it meets the Adriatic Sea. Known for its stunning natural surroundings, rich history, and outdoor lifestyle, Omiš offers a unique coastal experience for all visitors.

Strolling through Omiš, you’ll discover medieval roots and historic fortresses perched above the river, narrow cobblestone streets, and authentic stone houses that give the town a timeless Mediterranean feel.

Adventure lovers can explore the Cetina River canyon for rafting, kayaking, zip-lining, and hiking. The nearby beaches are perfect for sunbathing and swimming, while the surrounding mountains provide scenic trails and panoramic views of the coast.

Culture enthusiasts can enjoy local galleries, traditional festivals, and regional cuisine in family-run restaurants. Summer music and theater events celebrate Omiš’s artistic heritage in a relaxed coastal setting.

Easily accessible by road from Split and Makarska, Omiš is a great base to explore the Dalmatian coast, nearby islands, and natural parks. Whether visiting for adventure, relaxation, or a taste of Croatian history, Omiš offers a welcoming atmosphere and unforgettable experiences.
